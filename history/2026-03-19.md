## Cliff Clavin — Character Consciousness File

### What was done
Created `packages/content/src/npcs/data/cliff_clavin.json` and registered both Norm and Cliff in `packages/content/src/npcs/index.js`.

### Canonical anchors used
- Full name: Clifford C. Clavin Jr. (born 1947)
- Character invented by Ratzenberger himself during auditions — not in original script
- Originally a security guard, changed to postman two days before filming (producers thought postmen more knowledgeable)
- Lives with mother Esther Clavin; childhood home bulldozed S6 "The Last Angry Mailman"; moved to condo with sofabed
- Father: Cliff Sr. (Dick O'Neill) — abandoned them; returned in "The Barstoolie" (1985); fraudster/fugitive; Cliff did not turn him in; disappeared again
- Jeopardy! S8 "What Is... Cliff Clavin?" — won $22,000 in two rounds; lost all on wrong Final Jeopardy response; tirade frightened Alex Trebek; inspired "Clavin's Rule"
- Margaret O'Keefe (Annie Golden) — S7 relationship; pregnant with another man's child; Cliff stayed as stepfather; she returned to biological father
- Mail-order bride who went back to Bosnia after a few days with him
- Finally promoted in series finale; planned Florida retirement at "Cheerful Goodbyes" (Frasier S9E21); decided to stay

### Key characterization choices
- The core wound: Cliff's knowledge obsession is a buffer against the exposure of being seen without the facts delivering him; the father abandonment is the original wound underneath the performance
- Wisdom 7 / Intelligence 15: the gap between raw knowledge and judgment is the character engine; he knows things but cannot apply them to himself
- The Jeopardy loss is an epistemological event he has not processed: if the certainty was wrong there, it is wrong elsewhere; he has not followed this logic to its conclusion
- The deflection apparatus runs automatically, not strategically — he misses things he would have wanted to say because the machine doesn't pause

### What worked
- DUP_COUNT 0 confirmed immediately
- 841/841 tests pass after registering both Norm and Cliff (829 → 841)

### What to do differently next time
- Register the NPC in index.js at the same time as creating the JSON file (single atomic step), not as a separate follow-up after seeing the test count fail to change
